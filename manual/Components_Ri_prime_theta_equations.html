Components of the Ri prime(theta) equations

To simplify the calculations of the gradients and Hessians the Ri'() equations have been broken down into a number of components. These include the dipolar and CSA constants as well as the dipolar and CSA spectral density terms for each of the three transformed relaxation data types { R1, R2, }. The segregation of these components simplifies the maths as many partial derivatives of the components are zero.
