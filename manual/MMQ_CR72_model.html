The MMQ CR72 model

This is the analytic CR72 model for 2-site exchange on most times scales (Section 10.3.4 on page ) extended for multiple types of multiple quantum data (MMQ) by Korzhnev et al. (2004a). It is selected by setting the model to `MMQ CR72'. The simple constraint pA > pB is used to halve the optimisation space, as both sides of the limit are mirror image spaces.
