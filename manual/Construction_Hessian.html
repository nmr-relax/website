Construction of the Hessian

Figure 13.2: The model-free Hessian kite - a demonstration of the construction of the model-free Hessian for the global model . For each residue i a different matrix is constructed. The first element of the matrix represented by the two symbols (the red block) is the sub-matrix of chi-squared second partial derivatives with respect to the diffusion tensor parameters and . The orange blocks are the sub-matrices of chi-squared second partial derivatives with respect to the diffusion parameter and the model-free parameter . The yellow blocks are the sub-matrices of chi-squared second partial derivatives with respect to the model-free parameters and . For the residue dependent matrix the second partial derivatives with respect to the model-free parameters and where il are zero. In addition, the second partial derivatives with respect to the model-free parameters and where il are also zero. These blocks of sub-matrices are left uncoloured. The complete Hessian of is the sum of the matrices .
