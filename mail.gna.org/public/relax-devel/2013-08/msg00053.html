cd /usr/local/

 scons

Try instead as a normal user:

$ cd ~
$ mkdir relax
$ cd relax
$ svn co http://svn.gna.org/svn/relax/branches/relax_disp
$ cd relax_disp
$ /usr/local/bin/python /usr/local/bin/scons

Maybe this will work better.
