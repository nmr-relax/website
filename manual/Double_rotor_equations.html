Double rotor equations

The double rotor model consists of two standard rotations, the first about the x-axis and the second about the y-axis. Hence the frame order matrix is simply the integration over both torsion angles of the Kronecker product of the product of the Rx and Ry rotation matrices, divided by the surface area normalisation factor.
