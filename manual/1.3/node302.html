rdc.delete

Synopsis

Delete the RDC data corresponding to the alignment id.

Defaults

rdc.delete(self, id=None)

Keyword Arguments

id: The alignment identification string.

Examples

To delete the RDC data corresponding to id=`PH_gel', type:


relax> rdc.delete(`PH_gel')
