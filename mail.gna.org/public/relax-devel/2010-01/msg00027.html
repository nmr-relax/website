patch_import:

patch_stacking:

separated and I do not wish to do that for all your patches (it is a

procedure for software projects.  Once you can provide these
individual patches with acceptable commit messages, we will have a
vote to grant you commit access, and then the procedure for you to
make code changes will be much, much faster.  But first you need to
prove you can operate under the project rules.
