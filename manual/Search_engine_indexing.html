Search engine indexing

Having a large web of links across relax's infrastructure aids in the search engine indexing of the mailing list archives and the http://www.nmr-relax.comhttp://www.nmr-relax.com web site. The web of links is useful for catching those Google bots. That way the Google searching of the mailing list archives located on the http://www.nmr-relax.com/communication.htmlcommunication web page will be more up to date. However to increase the search engine ranking of the web site, links to http://www.nmr-relax.comhttp://www.nmr-relax.com from external sites is required. This is one reason why relax can be found at a number of sites across the web:

Freecode:
New relax releases are announced not only on the https://mail.gna.org/public/relax-announce/relax-announce mailing list and on the relax https://gna.org/news/?group=relaxnews pages, but also on http://freecode.com/projects/nmr-relaxFreecode. This used to be called Freshmeat.
The mail archive:
This site archives all of the relax mailing lists, including http://mail-archive.com/relax-announce@gna.org/relax-announce, http://mail-archive.com/relax-users@gna.org/relax-users, http://mail-archive.com/relax-devel@gna.org/relax-devel, and http://mail-archive.com/relax-commits@gna.org/relax-commits.
Gmane:
Pronounced as ``main'', the relax mailing lists are also archived at Gmane in numerous formats. The archived relax mailing lists include relax-announce (http://news.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.announcethread, http://blog.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.announceblog, nntp://news.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.announceNNTP, http://rss.gmane.org/messages/excerpts/gmane.science.nmr.relax.announceRSS), relax-users (http://news.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.userthread, http://blog.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.userblog, nntp://news.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.userNNTP, http://rss.gmane.org/messages/excerpts/gmane.science.nmr.relax.userRSS), relax-devel (http://news.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.develthread, http://blog.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.develblog, nntp://news.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.develNNTP, http://rss.gmane.org/messages/excerpts/gmane.science.nmr.relax.develRSS), and relax-commits (http://news.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.scmthread, http://blog.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.scmblog, nntp://news.gmane.org/gmane.science.nmr.relax.scmNNTP, http://rss.gmane.org/messages/excerpts/gmane.science.nmr.relax.scmRSS).
