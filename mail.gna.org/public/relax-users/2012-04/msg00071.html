Posted by Edward d'Auvergne on April 30, 2012 - 14:49:
Dear all,

If you have sent a message to the relax mailing lists between the afternoon of Wednesday the 25th and today, you probably received a delivery failure error message.  This was a problem with the Gna! mail server, which has now been fixed.  See https://gna.org/support/?2907 and https://gna.org/support/?2906 for details.

Most messages sent between the 25th and 30th have probably been permanently lost.  But if you see some text such at "YOU DO NOT NEED TO RESEND YOUR MESSAGE" in the mail delivery message, then you do not need to resend it as the message delivery to the mailing list will be attempted again later.  If you see text such as "Delivery to the following recipient failed permanently", could you please resend your message to the list?  Everything should now be working again.  Cheers!

Regards,

Edward
