Details:

The model of the Tollinger/Kay dispersion model from 2001 is wished as part of
the relax disp branch.

References:
Slow Dynamics in Folded and Unfolded States of an SH3 Domain.

Martin Tollinger , Nikolai R. Skrynnikov , Frans A. A. Mulder , Julie D.
Forman-Kay ,and Lewis E. Kay.  
J. Am. Chem. Soc., 2001, 123 (46)
http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ja011300z 


The inverted chevron plot measured by NMR relaxation reveals a native-like
unfolding intermediate in acyl-CoA binding protein.

Kaare Teilum, Flemming M Poulsen, Mikael Akke.
Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of
America (2006).
http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC1458987/figure/F1/


The model to minimize would be:
model = R2+ka*(1.0-sin(Domega*tau_cpmg)/(Domega*tau_cpmg))
