model_free.select_model()

Synopsis

Function for the selection of a preset model-free model.

Defaults

model_free.select_model(self, model=None, spin_id=None)

Keyword Arguments

model: The name of the preset model.

The preset models

The standard preset model-free models are

`m0' = {},
`m1' = {S2},
`m2' = {S2 , },
`m3' = {S2 , Rex },
`m4' = {S2 , , Rex },
`m5' = {S2f , S2 , },
`m6' = {S2f , , S2 , },
`m7' = {S2f , S2 , , Rex },
`m8' = {S2f , , S2 , , Rex },
`m9' = {Rex}.

The preset model-free models with optimisation of the CSA value are

`m10' = {CSA},
`m11' = {CSA, S2 },
`m12' = {CSA, S2 , },
`m13' = {CSA, S2 , Rex },
`m14' = {CSA, S2 , , Rex },
`m15' = {CSA, S2f , S2 , },
`m16' = {CSA, S2f , , S2 , },
`m17' = {CSA, S2f , S2 , , Rex },
`m18' = {CSA, S2f , , S2 , , Rex },
`m19' = {CSA, Rex }.

The preset model-free models with optimisation of the bond length are

`m20' = {r},
`m21' = {r , S2 },
`m22' = {r , S2 , },
`m23' = {r , S2 , Rex },
`m24' = {r , S2 , , Rex },
`m25' = {r , S2f , S2 , },
`m26' = {r , S2f , , S2 , },
`m27' = {r , S2f , S2 , , Rex },
`m28' = {r , S2f , , S2 , , Rex },
`m29' = {r , CSA, Rex }.

The preset model-free models with both optimisation of the bond length and CSA are

`m30' = {r , CSA},
`m31' = {r , CSA, S2 },
`m32' = {r , CSA, S2 , },
`m33' = {r , CSA, S2 , Rex },
`m34' = {r , CSA, S2 , , Rex },
`m35' = {r , CSA, S2f , S2 , },
`m36' = {r , CSA, S2f , , S2 , },
`m37' = {r , CSA, S2f , S2 , , Rex },
`m38' = {r , CSA, S2f , , S2 , , Rex },
`m39' = {r , CSA, Rex }.

Warning: The models in the thirties range fail when using standard R1 , R2 , and NOE relaxation data. This is due to the extreme flexibly of these models where a change in the parameter `r' is compensated by a corresponding change in the parameter `csa' and vice versa.

Additional preset model-free models, which are simply extensions of the above models with the addition of a local parameter are:

`tm0' = {tm},
`tm1' = { , S2 },
`tm2' = { , S2 , },
`tm3' = { , S2 , Rex },
`tm4' = { , S2 , , Rex },
`tm5' = { , S2f , S2 , },
`tm6' = { , S2f , , S2 , },
`tm7' = { , S2f , S2 , , Rex },
`tm8' = { , S2f , , S2 , , Rex },
`tm9' = { , Rex }.

The preset model-free models with optimisation of the CSA value are

`tm10' = { , CSA},
`tm11' = { , CSA, S2 },
`tm12' = { , CSA, S2 , },
`tm13' = { , CSA, S2 , Rex },
`tm14' = { , CSA, S2 , , Rex },
`tm15' = { , CSA, S2f , S2 , },
`tm16' = { , CSA, S2f , , S2 , },
`tm17' = { , CSA, S2f , S2 , , Rex },
`tm18' = { , CSA, S2f , , S2 , , Rex },
`tm19' = { , CSA, Rex }.

The preset model-free models with optimisation of the bond length are

`tm20' = { , r },
`tm21' = { , r , S2 },
`tm22' = { , r , S2 , },
`tm23' = { , r , S2 , Rex },
`tm24' = { , r , S2 , , Rex },
`tm25' = { , r , S2f , S2 , },
`tm26' = { , r , S2f , , S2 , },
`tm27' = { , r , S2f , S2 , , Rex },
`tm28' = { , r , S2f , , S2 , , Rex },
`tm29' = { , r , CSA, Rex }.

The preset model-free models with both optimisation of the bond length and CSA are

`tm30' = { , r , CSA},
`tm31' = { , r , CSA, S2 },
`tm32' = { , r , CSA, S2 , },
`tm33' = { , r , CSA, S2 , Rex },
`tm34' = { , r , CSA, S2 , , Rex },
`tm35' = { , r , CSA, S2f , S2 , },
`tm36' = { , r , CSA, S2f , , S2 , },
`tm37' = { , r , CSA, S2f , S2 , , Rex },
`tm38' = { , r , CSA, S2f , , S2 , , Rex },
`tm39' = { , r , CSA, Rex }.

Spin identification string

If `spin_id' is supplied then the model will only be selected for the corresponding spins. Otherwise the model will be selected for all spins.

Examples

To pick model `m1' for all selected spins, type:


relax> model_free.select_model(`m1')


relax> model_free.select_model(model=`m1')
