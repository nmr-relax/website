The extended model-free gradient

The model-free gradient of the extended spectral density function (15.63) is the vector of partial derivatives of the function with respect to the geometric parameter , the orientational parameter , the order parameters S2 and S2f, and the internal correlation times τf and τs. The positions in the vector correspond to the model parameters which are being optimised.
