On 11-02-01 4:37 PM, Edward d'Auvergne wrote:


Hi,

This is quite a common problem of things getting out of sync - I
have
this often in my checked out copy of the branches.  Just make a
backup
copy of all your changes, if you have any, and then delete the
'manual' directory, run 'svn cleanup', and then finally 'svn up'.
 You
can then copy back your changes.  This should fix it.  The exact
cause
of this is difficult to pinpoint.
