Figure 16.23: The free rotor pseudo-ellipse model simulated and calculated in-frame Daeg(1) frame order matrix elements. In these plots, θX corresponds to the cone opening half-angle θx and θY to the cone opening half-angle θy. When the half-angle is not varied, the angle is fixed to either θx = π/4 or θy = 3π/8. Frame order matrix values have been calculated every 10 degrees. The first angle for the calculated elements is set to 0.01 degrees as a pseudo-ellipse cone opening angle of 0.0 cannot be correctly handled by the numerical integration.
