structure.mean

Synopsis

Calculate the mean structure from all loaded models.

Defaults

structure.mean()

Description

This will calculate the mean structure from all loaded models. The mean structure will replace all the models in the internal structural object. This is provided as a structural aid, specifically for superimposition purposes.
