a list dedicated to major announcements relating to the program relax,
including new version releases or other important notices.  As such,
the volume of messages will be quite low when compared to the other
relax mailing lists.  The announcement list is <relax-announce at
gna.org> and can be subscribed to by visiting the list information
page at https://mail.gna.org/listinfo/relax-announce/.
