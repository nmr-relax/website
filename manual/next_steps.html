The next steps

This chapter presented the basics of setting up the relax data store, concepts which are needed for all analysis types built into relax. The next chapters will introduce specific analyses types - the steady-state NOE, R1 and R2 relaxation curve-fitting, and the automated full model-free analysis protocol of d'Auvergne and Gooley (2008b,2007) - which build on the ideas introduced here.
