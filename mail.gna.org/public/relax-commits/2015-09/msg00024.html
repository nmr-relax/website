+\subsection{Computation times}
+
+It should be noted that computation times are currently very long, in the 
order of months.
+This depends on the models chosen, the amount and quality of the input data, 
and the refinement step.
+This is due to the numeric integration for the PCS data.
+Calculations can range from one to six months on a very fast computer.
+Unfortunately parallelization was not possible, but multiple analyses can be 
performed simultaneously on multiple core or multiple CPU systems.
+A dedicated machine with a reliable UPS system is highly recommended.
