Hi,

This is an easy one.  What you describe is called teeing
(http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tee_%28command%29).  The output is split
into two - one goes to a file, the other to the screen.  If you run:

$ relax --help

you will see that there is a tee option ready for you to use :)

Edward
