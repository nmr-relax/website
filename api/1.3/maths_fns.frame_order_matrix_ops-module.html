  • A (numpy 5D, rank-1 array) - The full alignment tensor in {Axx, Ayy, Axy, Axz, Ayz} notation.
  • red_tensor (numpy 5D, rank-1 array) - The structure in {Axx, Ayy, Axy, Axz, Ayz} notation to place the reduced alignment tensor.
