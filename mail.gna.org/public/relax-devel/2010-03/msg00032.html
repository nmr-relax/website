Hi,

This is actually an email alias.  I set it up to direct emails to
another email address you provide to me in private.  To have
mailboxes, etc., I would need to send a lot of money to Godaddy, the
current provider.  As relax generates no money itself, that would have
to be my own money.  The current subscription allows for 100 free
email aliases, but that's about it.  No mailboxes, no website hosting
(http://nmr-relax.com is actually on the Gna! servers), etc.  You
could create a Gmail, Yahoo, etc. account for this purpose and have
the email alias point to there, or just use your Gmx account for now.

response to this message.  I.e. bieri att nmr-relax dott com,
michael.bieri att nmr-relax dott com, etc. (note how these are written
to stop spam).  You can send the email account name that the alias
