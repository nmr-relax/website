Then the axes of the diffusion are calculated, as unit vectors scaled to the appropriate length (multiplied by the eigenvalue Dx, Dy, Dz, Dpar, Dper, or Diso as well as the scale value), and a C atom placed at the position of this vector plus the centre of mass.
