Model selection references

The citation for the model selection component of relax is:

  • d'Auvergne and Gooley (2003)

The base citations for the specific model selection techniques of AIC, AICc, and BIC are respectively Akaike (1973); Schwarz (1978); Hurvich and Tsai (1989)

  • Akaike (1973)
  • Hurvich and Tsai (1989)
  • Schwarz (1978)
