rdc.display

Synopsis

Display the RDC data corresponding to the alignment id.

Defaults

rdc.display(self, id=None)

Keyword Arguments

id: The alignment identification string.

Examples

To display the `phage' RDC data, type:


relax> rdc.display(`phage')
