Hi James,

Welcome to the relax mailing lists!  Fortunately, your problem has
already been solved.  If you find a bug or problem in relax, it is
best to first check the relax bug tracker to see if the issue has
already been encountered (see https://gna.org/bugs/?group=relax).  In
this case, the bug that you see has been reported at
https://gna.org/bugs/?20197.  If you also look at the announcement for
relax 2.1.2 (https://gna.org/forum/forum.php?forum_id=2369), you will
see that the fix for this bug is included in this release.  So you
simply have to upgrade to relax 2.1.2 and your problem will be solved.

Regards,

Edward
