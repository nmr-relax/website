Model selection references

The citation for the model selection component of relax is:

  • d'Auvergne and Gooley (2003)

The base citations for the specific model selection techniques of AIC, AICc, and BIC are respectively Hurvich and Tsai (1989); Schwarz (1978); Akaike (1973)

  • Akaike (1973)
  • Hurvich and Tsai (1989)
  • Schwarz (1978)
