If these forms of the model-free spectral density functions are unfamiliar, that is because these are the numerically stabilised forms presented in d'Auvergne and Gooley (2008b). The original model-free spectral density functions presented in Lipari and Szabo (1982a) and Clore et al. (1990) are not the most numerically stable form of these equations.
