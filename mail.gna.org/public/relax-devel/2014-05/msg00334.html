Posted by Edward d'Auvergne on May 15, 2014 - 10:48:
Hi Troels,

I just wanted to point out something about your recent additions to
the relax wiki at
http://wiki.nmr-relax.com/B14#Comments_from_the_Author.  The part
about off-resonance effects in the CPMG is also listed at the top of
the relaxation dispersion 'to do' section of the manual
(http://www.nmr-relax.com/manual/do_dispersion_features_yet_be_implemented.html).

Note that the addition of such a feature to the numeric CPMG models
would need significant testing against the CATIA software from
Flemming Hansen, i.e. the CATIA data input creation and CATIA control
user functions in relax would need improvement, and some good test
data sets.  It should be put into a different numeric model as the
evolution matrix will be different.  That would also allow rigorous
comparisons of the off-resonance effect in the CPMG.  However I don't
expect that this feature will be added to relax in the near future,
despite its clear benefits for the final results.

Regards,

Edward
