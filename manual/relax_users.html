relax-users

If you would like to ask questions about relax, discuss certain features, receive help, or to communicate on any other subject related to relax the mailing list ``relax-users at gna.org'' is the place to post your message. To subscribe to the list go to the relax-users information page at https://web.archive.org/web/https://mail.gna.org/listinfo/relax-users/. You can also browse the mailing list archives at https://web.archive.org/web/https://mail.gna.org/public/relax-users/.
