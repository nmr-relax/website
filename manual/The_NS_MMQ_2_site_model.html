The NS MMQ 2-site model

This is the numerical model for 2-site exchange for proton-heteronuclear SQ, ZQ, DQ and MQ CPMG data, as derived in (Korzhnev et al., 2004b,a,2005a). It is selected by setting the model to `NS MMQ 2-site'. The simple constraint pA > pB is used to halve the optimisation space, as both sides of the limit are mirror image spaces. Different sets of equations are used for the different data types.
