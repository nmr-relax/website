Posted by Edward d'Auvergne on August 29, 2013 - 18:24:
Hi,

The clustering 'free spins' structure is not really a structure.  It
is really everything that does not have a cluster ID.  From the
information from the first analysis without clustering, you will be
able to determine the spins which do not experience Rex.  You would
then deselect these in a clustering analysis to avoid unnecessary
calculations.  There is quite a lot of manual interaction in a
relaxation dispersion analysis!  This is not relax specific.

Edward
