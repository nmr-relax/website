pcs.delete()

Synopsis

Delete the PCS data corresponding to the alignment ID.

Defaults

pcs.delete(self, align_id=None)

Keyword Arguments

align_id: The alignment ID string.

Examples

To delete the PCS data corresponding to align_id=`PH_gel', type:


relax> pcs.delete(`PH_gel')
