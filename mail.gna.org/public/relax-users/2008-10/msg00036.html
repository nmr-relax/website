The random coil region is the area around 8 ppm (+/- 0.5 ppm) in the
proton dimension where the protons of protein backbone amides located
in coils, rather than ordered alpha or beta secondary structure, lye.
Are your 1D experiments of proteins, RNA, DNA, or small molecules?  I
would guess that this effect will occur anywhere where there are many
intense signals, such as a solvent signal.
