On 11 January 2011 13:41, Edward d'Auvergne<edward@xxxxxxxxxxxxx>  wrote:

Hi,

Happy new year to you too!  That's a good idea - I can't keep up with
everything and didn't notice that it was not listed.  Would you like
to make the changes yourself?  You just need to checkout
svn.gna.org/svn/relax/website with your Gna! login and modify the
relevant webpages (the feature and reference pages, maybe index.html
as well).  Check the local files first in your web browser to make
sure they are valid - they will need to pass http://validator.w3.org/
with no errors.

Finally, make sure to include in the commit messages links to the
http://nmr-relax.com web pages (i.e.
http://www.nmr-relax.com/features.html,
http://www.nmr-relax.com/refs.html, http://nmr-relax.com).  The
purpose of this linking to http://nmr-relax.com is to increase the
Google and other web search ranking of http://nmr-relax.com ;)  The
more places http://nmr-relax.com appears on the web, the easier it is
for others to find relax.

Cheers,

Edward
