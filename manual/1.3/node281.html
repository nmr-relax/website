pcs.display

Synopsis

Display the PCS data corresponding to the alignment id.

Defaults

pcs.display(self, id=None)

Keyword Arguments

id: The alignment identification string.

Examples

To display the `phage' PCS data, type:


relax> pcs.display(`phage')
