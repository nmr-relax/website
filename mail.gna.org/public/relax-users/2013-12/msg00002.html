Dear all,

For those not subscribed to the very low traffic relax-announce
mailing list (https://mail.gna.org/listinfo/relax-announce/), to the
news feed (https://gna.org/news/atom.php?group=relax), or to the
freecode announcements (http://freecode.com/projects/nmr-relax), you
may have missed a few recent relax releases (3.0.2, 3.1.0, and 3.1.1).

Importantly are the new 3.1 releases which feature SÃ©bastien Morin's
relaxation dispersion branch.  This branch has been in development for
four and a half years and has been a collaboration between many groups
from all around the world.  This includes support for the greatest
range of dispersion models of all dispersion software, a graphical
user interface, parallelisation with OpenMPI for huge speed increases,
and many other unique features (see
http://gna.org/forum/forum.php?forum_id=2421 and
http://gna.org/forum/forum.php?forum_id=2423).  If you wish to be kept
informed about new relax developments in the future, please consider
subscribing to the relax-announce mailing list at
https://mail.gna.org/listinfo/relax-announce/.

Regards,
