integration of the Bloch-McConnell equations for the analysis of relaxation
dispersion data.  It is related to task #6401 (https://gna.org/task/?6401),

branch of relax
(http://svn.gna.org/viewcvs/relax/branches/relax_disp/?pathrev=20261).  It
will be used as the central point for the development and addition of this
