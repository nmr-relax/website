Joining the relax project

The second step in becoming a committer is to register to become a member of the relax project. Go to the Gna! website (https://web.archive.org/web/https://gna.org/) and login. Click on `My Groups' to go to https://web.archive.org/web/https://gna.org/my/groups.php. In the section `Request for inclusion' type `relax' and hit enter. Select relax and write something in the comments. If you have been approved (see section 13.4.1) you will be added to the project committers list.
