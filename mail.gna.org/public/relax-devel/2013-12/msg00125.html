Details:

As reported on the devel ml, some png suffer from broken profiles. those can
be identified y running 'find relax-3.1.1/ -name "*.png" -exec identify '{}'
\; > /dev/null'

and the fix is

pngcrush -fix -force old.png new.png

Please find attached the fixed versions of the icons.
