Log:
More synthetic NMR relaxation data for the model-free system tests of the 
test suite.

The data was back calculated from the model-free parameters:
    S2f = 0.952,
    S2s = 0.582,
    ts = 32 ns.

The synthetic relaxation data is noise free and consists of the R1, R2, and 
NOE at both 500 and
600 MHz.
