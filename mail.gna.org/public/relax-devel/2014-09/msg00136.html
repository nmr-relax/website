Posted by Edward d'Auvergne on September 08, 2014 - 13:35:
That's quite an interesting idea!  There is nothing in relax for this.
If you are interested in implementing this, have a look at the status
object.  This is a temporary object, it is not saved in the state
files.  That is probably the best place for this, just create a list
that expands as the analysis goes on.  The relax data store is
probably not appropriate for such information.  It might be
complicated to implement though as this will require low level work
and the concept of the relax library independence will complicate the
design.  Would just the text of the warning be enough, or are you
after more?  Do you have any ideas for an implementation?
