Log:
Renamed the 'MQ CR72' dispersion model to 'MMQ CR72'.

The model is designed by Korzhnev et al., 2004 for proton-heteronuclear SQ, 
ZQ, DQ, and MQ data
(or MMQ data), so the change is logical as the model is not just for MQ data.
