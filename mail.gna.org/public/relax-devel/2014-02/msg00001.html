Hi Edward,

I noticed that the NOE is not calculated anymore in the latest relax version
(I just checked out r22114). The noe.out file consists only of "None" values.
Relax warns:

relax> calc(verbosity=1)

Over-fit spin deselection:
RelaxWarning: The spin '#XYZ_mol1:120@N' has been deselected because of
insufficient data.

But I'm not sure why it deselects every residue and what the criterion is to
deselect any residue.

The relax 2.2.4 I keep around in case of an issue with a new release
calculates it correctly, my guess is that this is also true for earlier 3.x
versions.

I attached a minimal dataset with the standard calculation procedure for
calculation of non-replicated spectra with error estimation from baseplane
RMSD.
