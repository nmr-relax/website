Construction of the gradient

Figure 15.1: The construction of the model-free gradient ∇χ2 for the global model . For each residue i a different vector ∇χ2i is constructed. The first element of the vector represented by the symbol ∂ (the orange block) is the sub-vector of chi-squared partial derivatives with respect to each of the diffusion tensor parameters . The rest of the elements, grouped into blocks for each residue denoted by the symbol ∂, are the sub-vectors of chi-squared partial derivatives with respect to each of the model-free parameters . For the residue dependent vector ∇χ2i the partial derivatives with respect to the model-free parameters of where i≠j are zero. These blocks are left uncoloured. The complete gradient of is the sum of the vectors ∇χ2i.
