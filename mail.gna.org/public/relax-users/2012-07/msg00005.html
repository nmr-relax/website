Hi Romel,

I have now fixed this bug in relax.  You can download the repository
copy of relax as I described before, if you wish.  However the fix is
simply to warn you that the peak lists are inconsistent (with a
RelaxError).  Therefore you could instead just make sure your peak
lists have the same number of peaks and use relax 2.0.0.  I would
recommend that you take your peak lists that you have shifted to the
centre with the Sparky 'pc' command, and create one averaged list
(averaging the chemical shift).  You should then use this averaged
list for determining the peak heights in all spectra.  This avoids the
problem of a double exponential fit due to the non-constant RMDS of
the baseplane noise across your spectra.  And it is a better technique
for peaks which disappear into the baseplane noise.

Regards,

Edward
