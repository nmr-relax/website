Details:

A chi2 hypersurface was constructed over a map of 'dw', 'pA', 'kex'.

It is exemplified at.
http://wiki.nmr-relax.com/Dx_map

Data was created with:
model_create = MODEL_NS_CPMG_2SITE_EXPANDED
And minimised with:
model_analyse = MODEL_CR72

sfrq_1 = 599.8908617*1E6
time_T2_1 = 0.06
ncycs_1 = [28, 4, 32, 60, 2, 10, 16, 8, 20, 50, 18, 40, 6, 12, 24]

sfrq_2 = 499.8908617*1E6
time_T2_2 = 0.04
ncycs_2 = [20, 16, 10, 36, 2, 12, 4, 22, 18, 40, 14, 26, 8, 32, 24, 6, 28 ]

With spin dynamics created with:
'r2' = 20 rad /s.
'kex' = 2200.0
'dw' = 1.5.

The chi2 surface mapped on the 3 dimensions. shows a highly alarming profile.
