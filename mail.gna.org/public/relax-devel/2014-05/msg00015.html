Posted by Edward d'Auvergne on May 02, 2014 - 09:13:
Hi Troels,

Here the model really must be between the 'TSMFK01' CPMG model
(http://wiki.nmr-relax.com/TSMFK01) and 'NS CPMG 2-site 3D' CPMG model
(http://wiki.nmr-relax.com/NS_CPMG_2-site_3D).  Here the ordering is:

- R2eff models,
- Analytic CPMG models,
- Numeric CPMG models,
- Analytic R1rho models,
- Numeric R1rho models.

In each of these sections, the ordering is by date.  So the B14 model
(http://wiki.nmr-relax.com/B14) should be the last of the analytic
CPMG models.  In the future, I might introduce some type of divider
between these 5 major model categories.

Cheers,

Edward
